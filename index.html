3D printing service based in Singapore.

We offer on-demand FDM 3D printing for prototypes, functional parts, replacement components, and custom models. Whether you have a ready-to-print file or just a concept in mind, we can help bring your idea to life — with fast turnaround and competitive pricing.

How to order

01
Fill in the form. Click here for job form. If you need CAD modelling, send explicit instructions or technical drawings to the same form. You may optionally include your preferred infill percentage, infill type, and print orientation. Please include your full name, delivery address, and phone number in the form.
02
Receive a quotation. We'll reply within 2 working days (excluding weekends and public holidays) with a quotation for your job. The quote will include the exact amount and a bank payment QR code.
03
Pay and confirm. To proceed, pay using the bank QR code and email a screenshot of the payment to the same address. Your part will be printed and shipped within 1 working week, or 3 days + print time — whichever is longer. For large-scale jobs, we'll provide a custom timeline.
